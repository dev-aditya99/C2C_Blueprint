Interactive 5-Month Roadmap

Our path to launch is meticulously planned. This section details the objectives and key results for each month. Click on a month to explore the specific tasks and responsibilities that will drive our progress from foundation to scale.

October: Foundation & Strategic Planning

  • Finalize Product Roadmap and MVP feature set.
  • Complete market & competitor analysis for Jaipur.
  • Establish tech stack, architecture, and security protocols.
  • Define founder roles: CPO/CTO and CEO/CMO.
  • Handle legal incorporation and financial structuring.

November: MVP Development & Design

  • Develop backend APIs for users, listings, and payments.
  • Build responsive web and mobile app frontends.
  • Integrate secure payment gateway and logistics partners.
  • Conduct internal alpha testing and bug fixing sprints.
  • Finalize UI/UX designs and user flow.

December: Pre-Launch Marketing & Community Building

  • Launch pre-launch landing page and waitlist campaign.
  • Initiate hyperlocal social media marketing in Jaipur.
  • Engage with local tech influencers and campus ambassadors.
  • Secure initial PR and media coverage.
  • Aim for 5,000+ waitlist sign-ups.

January: Platform Launch & Initial Traction

  • Official launch of Woovle web and mobile apps in Jaipur.
  • Onboard waitlist users and seed initial listings.
  • Monitor KPIs: user activation, listing creation, transactions.
  • Gather user feedback through surveys and support channels.
  • Implement initial revenue models (commissions & featured listings).

February: Optimization & Scalability Planning

  • Analyze user data and feedback to prioritize feature updates.
  • Optimize app performance, UI/UX, and user flows.
  • Refine marketing strategy based on launch data.
  • Develop a strategic plan for expansion to the next city.
  • Begin preliminary talks for seed funding rounds.
