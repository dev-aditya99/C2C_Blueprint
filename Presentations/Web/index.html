Business Model & Revenue

Our financial viability is built on a diversified and scalable revenue model, ensuring resilience and multiple income avenues. This section breaks down our primary revenue streams, from core transaction commissions to value-added services that enhance the user experience.

Commission Fees

The core of our model. We take a small percentage of every successful sale and rental transaction, directly aligning our success with our users'.

Premium Listings

Sellers and hosts can pay a fee to feature their listings, gaining increased visibility and priority placement in search results for faster transactions.

Targeted Advertising

Leveraging user data, we can offer targeted advertising opportunities for relevant businesses seeking to reach our engaged consumer base.

Value-Added Services

Offering optional paid services like professional item inspection, enhanced cleaning, or streamlined logistics facilitation.

Insurance Plans

For high-value rentals, we will offer optional, platform-backed protection plans to mitigate risk for both owners and renters, generating an additional revenue stream.

Subscription Tiers

Future potential for power-sellers or businesses to subscribe to monthly plans for reduced fees, advanced analytics, and dedicated support.
